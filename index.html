ABOUT ME

Boma Okoloko (alias BomsyOk) is a Certified Holistic Skin care Therapist, Coach and Organic Cosmetics Formulator. She is The Founder of Bomsy's Natural SkincareⓇ Products, Services and Holistic Consultation which has a vision to help people especially middle aged women like herself take control of their general health and wellbeing with emphasis on their beauty and personal Selfcare.

She runs several Platforms such as Torch Bearers Ladies Bible Study, Bomsy Holistic Wellbeing Community, Godly Heritage Teens and Youth Discipleship where she has a vision to help women to become purposeful and successful in their endeavors as well as raising a new generation of African youths and teens who are strong and holistically balanced.

With her background in Business Administration/Management, Boma is also the Head of Administration and Facilitator for the Grace Life Church Discipleship Training Program, Lagos Nigeria.She is married to Omoefe and the union is blessed with children.
